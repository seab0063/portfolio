About Me

I was born at the Ottawa General Hospital on July 21st, 2002 and have lived in the city for my entire life. My dad was born in Grimsby, Ontario where most of my family lives around and came to Ottawa when he started university at Carleton, and my mom lived in Cornwall until she started college at Algonquin. I believe my family has always been in Canada for many generations, so all I can say about my nationality is that I'm Canadian. I started school in 2006 at Arch Street Public School for pre-kindergarten when I was four. I don't remember much from that year but what I do know is that I still talk to a few of my classmates from back then to this day.
